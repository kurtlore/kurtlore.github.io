ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Kurt Canto, born October 18, 2006, thinks that writing in the third person is unsettling. Jokes aside, he is a diehard fan of Taylor, Olivia, Lorde, Hozier, Phoebe, and Sufjan. His whole personality revolves around music, books, and fictional characters. His personal holy trinity is Folklore, Reputation, and Speak Now, and his most streamed song of 2021 is 'Dress.'
